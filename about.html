About the Artist

Dev Sahrawat is a fifth grader and lives in California with his parents and older brother. He has always loved to draw from a very young age and practices it every day. When he is not drawing, he is observing and translating the world into his own "cartooney" style. He also enjoys reading, watching documentaries and cartoons, and playing basketball.
